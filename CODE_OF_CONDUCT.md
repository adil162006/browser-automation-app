# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free project for
everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience, or level of
participation.

## Expected Behavior

- Be considerate and constructive.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Give context, accept good-faith questions, and help others learn.
- Respect privacy, boundaries, and differing viewpoints.
- Take responsibility for mistakes and repair harm where possible.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Sexualized language or unwelcome attention.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Deliberate disruption, intimidation, or sustained bad-faith participation.
- Conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional community.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and may
temporarily or permanently restrict participation when this policy is violated.
Enforcement decisions should be proportionate, documented privately, and made
without retaliation.

## Reporting

Do not report conduct incidents in a public issue. Contact the repository
maintainers privately through the repository owner's available contact channel.
The repository does not currently publish a dedicated conduct email address.

Reports should include the relevant links, dates, context, and any requested
confidentiality. Maintainers should acknowledge reports promptly and protect the
privacy and safety of everyone involved.
